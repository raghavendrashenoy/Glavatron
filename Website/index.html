Server Side- CMS, Backend

3D Content Creation

iOT platform & hardware

IOT Platform – The internet of things or IoT, is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals or people that are provided with unique identifiers (UIDs) and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.

Face recognition based LMS

A facial recognition system uses biometrics to map facial features from a photograph or video. It compares the information with a database of known faces to find a match.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality (AR) is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real world are enhanced by computer-generated perceptual information

Fleet Management & Tracking

This technology uses GPS to monitor location, travel direction, and speed. Fleet GPS tracking is primarily used to monitor a collection of vehicles, but can also track drivers and other assets, such as packages.

Field Force Tracking

Field force tracking is a system that tracks down all your workforce activities in real time. Every task and event that is registered with web or mobile-based software can be monitored remotely with minimal complexities. Management can stay in touch with field force personnel and know their exact location on the map instantaneously, distance covered, status of the task, etc.

Remote Contact Centre Solution

A Video conferencing solution that provides the online connect to the offline dealerships. Provides the real-time walk-in without actually visiting the dealership. Consumers can access information and have face to face meetings, exchange collaterals, estimates and make bookings.

Digital Analytical dashboards

Digital analytics is the process of analyzing digital data from various sources like websites, mobile applications, among others. It provides a clear vision to the organization on how users or customers are behaving.
